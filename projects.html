Case study

PrestaShop e-commerce redesign

This project was a full redesign of an industrial B2B e-commerce website, with a clear objective: improve user experience, strengthen organic visibility and support revenue growth.

  • Role: Project leadership, coordination of stakeholders, performance follow-up and prioritization of business and digital actions.
  • Method: Work on UX, site architecture, SEO, conversion funnel, content and reporting tools such as GA4 and Looker Studio.
  • Result: E-commerce revenue increased from €355k to €540k, with stronger commercial performance and a clearer website structure.

Case study

SEO deployment and Stahlwerk product structuring

This project focused on growing organic visibility around a brand with very limited presence in the French market, using a content strategy, better site structure and targeted SEO improvements.

  • Role: SEO strategy definition, prioritization of categories, internal linking improvements and page structure optimization.
  • Method: Search intent analysis, content creation, category optimization for welding products and resolution of cannibalization issues.
  • Result: Better impressions, stronger ranking positions and a cleaner website structure built for long-term SEO growth [cite:4].
